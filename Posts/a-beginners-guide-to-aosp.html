Hello reader!,

How are you doing? In today's blog post you will receive a guide which will help you to get a better understanding of AOSP (Android Open Source Project) Before we get started let me explain what are the minimum requirements for AOSP development long story short.

So, according to the official documentation google uses 64-core computer yes you are reading right! with 64gb of ram (it's insane!) Which still takes 40 minutes for a full build other than you will need to have around 400gb of free space, I personally recommend you to have a dedicated 500gb SSD (Solid-State-Drive) for faster performance for those who don't know SSD's provide faster performance than HDD (Hard Disk-Drive) So, A question arrives here is it wort it to get those $1000 machines well to answer this just ask your self two questions -

Yes start to change things right up from the boot (Boot animation) this helps you to get an a-z customisation and also it helps you to keep thing organized. Looong Story short it helps you to save time and stay productive.
